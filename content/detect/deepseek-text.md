## Can You Detect DeepSeek Text?

Yes. DeepSeek is a powerful open-source AI model from China that has gained global popularity. While its English output quality rivals GPT-4, it carries detectable patterns — particularly in sentence rhythm, transition usage, and a tendency toward comprehensive explanations.

## DeepSeek's Writing Fingerprint

DeepSeek text often features:
- **Thorough explanations** — tends to cover every angle, even when brevity would suffice
- **Structured reasoning** — "First... Second... Third..." patterns
- **Technical precision** — even in casual topics, vocabulary skews formal
- **Balanced paragraphs** — consistent length across sections
