## GPT-4o's Writing Patterns

GPT-4o is more conversational than GPT-4 but still shows:
- **Even sentence pacing** — less variation than human writers
- **Polite hedging** — "That's a great question," "There are several perspectives"
- **Structured responses** — numbered lists, clear topic sentences
- **Vocabulary consistency** — same register throughout (no sudden shifts from formal to casual)
