## What Is an AI Generated Text Checker?

An AI generated text checker analyzes writing to determine whether it was produced by an AI model — such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or LLaMA — rather than written by a human.

Unlike basic plagiarism checkers, AI text checkers look for **statistical patterns** unique to language models: uniform sentence rhythm, overuse of transition words, predictable vocabulary choices, and low perplexity scores.

## How Our Checker Works

Our detector analyzes text at the **word level**, not just the paragraph level. Every word receives an AI-probability score. The result is a heatmap — red and orange words indicate high AI likelihood, green words look human.

This means you don't just get a number like "87% AI." You see **exactly which sentences** triggered the result, so you can fix them.
