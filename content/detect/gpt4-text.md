## Can You Detect GPT-4 Text?

Yes, though GPT-4 is harder to detect than GPT-3.5. GPT-4 produces more natural, varied text — but it still carries statistical patterns that distinguish it from human writing.

## GPT-4 vs GPT-3.5: Detection Differences

| Aspect | GPT-3.5 | GPT-4 |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Sentence variety | Low | Medium |
| Vocabulary range | Narrow | Broader |
| Detection rate | 90-95% | 85-92% |
| Common giveaway | Repetitive transitions | Overly balanced arguments |

GPT-4's main giveaway is **structural perfection** — every paragraph is well-formed, every argument has a counterpoint, every list has exactly 3-5 items. Real humans are messier.
