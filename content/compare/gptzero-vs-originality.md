## GPTZero: Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**
- Strong academic focus with classroom integrations
- Good accuracy on student essays
- Batch upload for teachers

**Weaknesses:**
- Free tier is very limited (5K characters)
- No word-level analysis — just a score and highlighted sentences
- No rewrite suggestions — tells you what's wrong but not how to fix it

## Originality.ai: Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**

- Combined AI + plagiarism detection
- Team management features

**Weaknesses:**
- No free tier at all — $15/month minimum
- Per-credit pricing can get expensive at scale
- No word-level heatmap
- Overkill for individual users
