## Why AI Content Detection Matters for Publishers

### Google's Stance on AI Content
Google has stated it rewards **helpful, high-quality content** regardless of how it was produced. However, content that is mass-produced, low-quality, or clearly generated without human oversight risks algorithmic demotion.

AI content detectors help you:
- Ensure content meets your quality bar before publishing
- Identify pieces that need more human editing and voice
- Protect your site's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust) signals

### Reader Trust
Audiences increasingly expect authentic, expert-driven content. AI-generated filler articles erode trust. Detection tools help maintain the human quality your readers expect.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can the detector handle long-form content like full articles?**
Yes. There's no hard limit. For very long pieces (5,000+ words), we recommend analyzing in sections for more granular results.

**Will it flag content that uses AI for research but is human-written?**
Generally no — if the final writing is done by a human in their own words, it will score low. The detector analyzes writing style, not topic knowledge.

**My human-written content scored high — why?**
Some writing styles naturally have lower perplexity (technical writing, formal reports, lists). If you write in a structured, formal style, you may score higher than average. Consider it a signal to add more varied, personal language.

**Can I use this to check my own AI-assisted drafts?**
Absolutely. Many writers use AI for initial drafts and then heavily edit. Run your draft through the detector to see if your editing has been sufficient.
