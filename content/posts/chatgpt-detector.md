## How Does a ChatGPT Detector Work?

A **ChatGPT detector** analyzes statistical patterns in text that are characteristic of GPT-based models:

- **Low perplexity** — ChatGPT produces predictable, "safe" word choices
- **High uniformity** — Sentence structures tend to be consistent and balanced
- **Neutral tone** — GPT models avoid strong opinions or emotional language unless prompted
- **Transitional phrases** — Overuse of "Furthermore," "In addition," "It's worth noting"

Our model is trained on millions of human-written and ChatGPT-generated samples to identify these patterns with high accuracy.

**Is this ChatGPT detector free?**
Yes — completely free, no signup, no word limit on basic checks.

**Can it tell the difference between GPT-3.5 and GPT-4?**
Our tool gives an overall AI probability score. Distinguishing between specific GPT versions is very difficult due to overlapping patterns, but we indicate confidence levels for different model families.

**What if the text was edited after being generated by ChatGPT?**
Heavily edited text will score lower. Minor edits usually don't affect the score significantly. The more the original AI output is rewritten, the harder it becomes to detect.

**How accurate is it?**
We achieve 93–96% accuracy on unmodified ChatGPT outputs. Accuracy decreases with heavy editing or humanization.
