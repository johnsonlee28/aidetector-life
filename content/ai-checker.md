## What Can an AI Checker Detect?

Our AI checker is trained to identify patterns typical of large language models (LLMs):

- **Perplexity** — AI text tends to be predictable and low-surprise
- **Burstiness** — Humans vary sentence length; AI text is more uniform
- **Repetitive phrasing** — AI models often reuse transitional phrases
- **Semantic flatness** — AI-generated text lacks the subtle nuances of human voice

## AI Checker vs. AI Detector — What's the Difference?

The terms are used interchangeably. An **AI checker** typically refers to a quick, lightweight scan, while an **AI detector** may imply a more thorough analysis. At AIDetector.life, we offer both: fast checks for quick verification and deep analysis for detailed reports.
