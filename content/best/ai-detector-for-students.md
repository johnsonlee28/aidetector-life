## Why Students Need an AI Detector

Whether you're using AI as a writing aid or want to make sure your own work doesn't get falsely flagged, you need a detector that's **accurate, private, and free**.

Most students can't afford $10-20/month for GPTZero or Originality.ai. And many school-provided tools (like Turnitin) aren't accessible outside the classroom.

## What Students Should Look For

1. **Free with no limits** — You'll check multiple drafts. Per-check pricing adds up fast.
2. **Word-level detail** — A percentage score doesn't help you fix anything. You need to see *which sentences* are flagged.
3. **Rewrite suggestions** — If sentences get flagged, you need to know how to rephrase them naturally.
4. **Privacy** — Your essays shouldn't be stored or used for training data.

## FAQ

### Will my teacher's AI detector flag the same things?
Different detectors use different methods. If our detector gives you a low AI score, most other tools will too. But no detector is 100% consistent with another.

### I didn't use AI but my essay got flagged — why?
This is a [false positive](/false-positive/). Formal, well-structured writing sometimes triggers AI detectors. Our heatmap shows you exactly which sentences to rephrase.

### Is my text stored after I check it?
No. We don't store, log, or use your text for any purpose.
