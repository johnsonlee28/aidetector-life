## 📋 提交描述（直接复制粘贴）

**Short (50 words):**
> Free AI content detector with word-level highlighting. Unlike tools that only give a percentage score, AI Detector shows exactly which words are flagged — so you can fix them. Supports ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini detection. No signup required.

**Long (150 words):**
> AI Detector is a free tool that checks whether text was written by AI — and tells you exactly where. While most detectors give you a single score like "87% AI generated," AI Detector highlights every word with a probability heat map, showing you precisely which phrases triggered the detection. This word-level transparency lets writers, students, and content creators understand and fix flagged content instead of guessing blindly. Features include: word-level heatmap highlighting, rewrite suggestions for flagged phrases, support for ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini/GPT-4 detection, and no signup required. Built for freelance writers worried about false positives, students submitting academic work, and content creators publishing online. Try it free at aidetector.life.
