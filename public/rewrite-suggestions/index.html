Detection Without Rewriting Is Half the Job#

Every AI detector can tell you that your text sounds AI-written. Almost none tell you what to do about it.

AIDetector.life does both. Paste your text, get an AI score — and for every flagged sentence, get a concrete rewrite suggestion that sounds more human.

How Rewrite Suggestions Work#

When you analyze your text, the tool:

  1. Identifies flagged sentences — sentences with AI scores above 50 are marked
  2. Generates a rewrite — each flagged sentence gets a suggested human-sounding alternative
  3. Shows a specific tip — tells you which pattern was detected and how to fix it
  4. Lets you compare — see the original and suggested version side by side

The result: you don’t just know your score. You know exactly what to change and have a starting point for how to change it.

What Gets Rewritten#

The rewrite engine targets the most common AI patterns:

Transition filler“Furthermore, it is important to…”“Also, …” / “On top of that, …”
Formal openers“In conclusion, we can see that…”“So, …” / “Bottom line: …”

This Is Not Auto-Humanizing#

Rewrite suggestions are starting points, not finished replacements.

Auto-humanizers like Undetectable.ai swap words algorithmically to fool detectors. The output often sounds awkward, loses meaning, or introduces errors.

Rewrite suggestions show you a direction: “this sentence uses AI filler — here’s a simpler alternative.” You decide whether to use the suggestion, modify it, or take a completely different approach. The writing stays yours.

Frequently Asked Questions#

Do I have to use the rewrite suggestion exactly? No. The suggestions are starting points. Use them as a direction and edit in your own voice. The goal is writing that sounds like you, not writing that sounds like a rewriter trying to sound like you.

What if I disagree with the rewrite? That’s fine. If a sentence was flagged but you wrote it yourself and want to keep it, you can. Use the suggestion to understand why it was flagged, then decide whether to change it.

How many sentences get rewrite suggestions? Up to 5 of the most heavily flagged sentences per analysis. Focusing on the top flagged sentences usually produces the biggest score reduction.

Will using the suggestions guarantee a lower score? The suggestions target the most common AI patterns. Using them typically lowers the score. But every piece of writing is different — re-analyze after editing to verify.

Can I use this for non-English text? Currently optimized for English. Non-English text may produce inaccurate results.
