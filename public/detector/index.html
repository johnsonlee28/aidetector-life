How This AI Detector Works

Our detector analyzes multiple linguistic signals associated with AI-generated text:

  • Perplexity — AI text uses predictable, statistically "safe" word choices. Human writing is more surprising.
  • Burstiness — Humans vary sentence length dramatically. AI text is more uniform.
  • AI Phrase Patterns — LLMs overuse phrases like "In conclusion," "It is worth noting," "Furthermore," etc.
  • Lexical Diversity — AI models tend to repeat similar vocabulary across a text.

Best results: Use texts of 100+ words. Short texts produce less reliable scores.
