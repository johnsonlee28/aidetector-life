### Description (About)
```
Most AI detectors give you a single percentage and stop there.
AIDetector.life goes deeper:

🌡️ Word-level heatmap — hover over any word to see its AI probability score. Not sentences. Words.

✍️ Rewrite suggestions — for every flagged sentence, get a human-sounding alternative instantly. It's a detector + humanizer in one.

📊 Transparent scoring — we show you every signal: AI phrases detected, sentence uniformity (CV), lexical diversity (TTR). No black box.

🆓 Actually free — no word limits, no sign-up, no paywall.

Built for teachers, editors, and anyone who needs to know not just "is this AI?" but "where exactly is the AI?"
```
